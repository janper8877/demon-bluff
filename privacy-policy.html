Demon Bluff Extension Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Demon Bluff Twitch Extension handles information when viewers interact with the Extension and when a streamer connects the Unity game to Twitch.

Information We Collect

The Extension and related backend server may process the following information:

  • Twitch channel ID of the streamer using the Extension.
  • Twitch user ID and Twitch display name for viewers who click the Join button or share their Twitch identity.
  • Votes submitted by viewers, including which card or cards were selected.
  • Temporary game state such as current round, dead cards, vote counts, leaderboard scores, and vote statistics.
  • Temporary Twitch authorization data when a streamer uses the Connect Twitch feature in Unity.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to operate the interactive gameplay features of the Extension, including:

  • Connecting a Twitch channel to the Unity game.
  • Allowing viewers to join and vote during a game round.
  • Displaying vote results, vote statistics, and leaderboard entries in Unity.
  • Separating game sessions between different streamers.

Data Storage

The backend currently stores gameplay data in server memory for the purpose of running active game sessions. The system is not designed to permanently store viewer profiles or long-term personal records. Data may be cleared when rounds are reset, leaderboards are reset, or the server restarts.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell viewer or streamer information. Information is used by the Extension, backend server, and Unity game integration only for the gameplay features described above. Information may be visible to the streamer inside the Unity game, for example as vote results or leaderboard entries.

Third-Party Services

The Extension uses Twitch services for Extension authorization and identity sharing. The backend may be hosted on Railway or a similar hosting provider. Twitch and hosting providers may process data according to their own policies.

Viewer Choice

Viewers can choose not to click Join or share their Twitch identity. If a viewer does not join, the Extension may not allow that viewer to participate in voting or leaderboard features.

Contact

For privacy questions about this Extension, contact the developer through the Demon Bluff Twitch channel: https://www.twitch.tv/demonbluff.
